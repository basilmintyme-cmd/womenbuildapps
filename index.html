About the community

A hobby club for the new era of building

We used to call it coding. Now, with AI tools, anyone can build. Women Build Apps is a non-profit community founded on that belief — that building apps is no longer just for traditional developers.

Share what you're making, ask questions when you're stuck, mentor others when you have experience. Every woman who builds can host, speak, and demo.
